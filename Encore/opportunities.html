Encore Dental Services

The Evolution of Practice Ownership

Encore Dental Services owns and manages vibrant, thriving dental practices, partnering with community-based dentists so those providers can prioritize patient care. Great patient care drives everything, and our goal is to relieve our partner dentists of operations and practice management so that they can focus on a service environment built first and foremost for the patient experience.

We go above and beyond a standard management company, providing multiple opportunities for our partner dentists to choose the path appropriate for them, whether based on stage of life or career.

The dental industry is evolving and the landscape is changing. If you’re a solo practitioner, or even a small group practice, you will benefit from having strong partners on your side. Now is the time!

Sell and Retain Material Ownership

Sell a portion of your practice and give up the headaches of practice management.

Buy into a Practice Partnership

Buy in as a Partner With Us – Often we purchase practices from dentists that are nearing retirement or for other reasons simply desire to sell the entire practice and transition out.

Sell Your Practice

Operate With Us – We can assist doctors who want to sell and walk away or sell and retain the right to continue to work in the practice as an employee dentist. This allows you to focus 100% of patient care without the hassles of business management. It also allows the dentist to not have to worry about the practice when they are away.

Partner on Multiple practices

This is for the entrepreneurial dentist who likes the business side of dentistry. We can assist you in putting a plan together for you to be part owner of multiple locations. Great way to save for retirement and maximize your passive income.
