Partnership and Associate Opportunities

Encore Dental Services

The Evolution of Practice Ownership

Encore Dental Services owns and manages vibrant, thriving dental practices, partnering with community-based dentists so those providers can prioritize patient care. Great patient care drives everything, and our goal is to relieve our partner dentists of operations and practice management so that they can focus on a service environment built first and foremost for the patient experience.

We go above and beyond a standard management company, providing multiple opportunities for our partner dentists to choose the path appropriate for them, whether based on stage of life or career.

  • Sell Your Practice
  • Sell and Retain Material Ownership in Partnership with Encore
  • Buy into a Practice Partnership
  • Partner on Multiple practices
  • Become an Associate in a Successful Practice

The dental industry is evolving and the landscape is changing. If you’re a solo practitioner, or even a small group practice, you will benefit from having strong partners on your side. Now is the time!

Harvest the value you’ve created! Expand your opportunities. Reduce your risk.

Sell Your Practice

Operate With Us – Sell a portion of your practice and give up the headaches of practice management. We typically purchase 60% - 80% from dentists that want to ensure they realize profits from their practice now as opposed to waiting…but want to remain with the practice and control clinical operations and patient care. In an evolving dental industry this can be a smart move. Retain a portion of your practice to be able to continue to enjoy the benefits of ownership and benefit from enhanced growth and profitability under the Encore umbrella.

Buy into a Practice

Buy In as a Partner With Us – Often we purchase practices from dentists that are nearing retirement or for other reasons simply desire to sell the entire practice and transition out. In those instances, we find highly qualified dentists to be our partners as quickly as we can. We firmly believe in doctor ownership as the best model for success for Encore and for our family of doctors.

Join the Associate Program and Grow into a Buyer

Associate Program – Grow into a partner; in the meantime, be with a dynamic organization that will help you grow and let you concentrate on patient care while learning as much as you want about best practices for practice management.
