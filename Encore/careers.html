Careers

Join The Team

We’re always looking for people who can bring new perspectives and life experiences to our teams. We’re always seeking colleagues who are big thinkers eager to take on fresh challenges as a team. If you’re smart, hungry and humble and looking for a place that values your curiosity, passion, and desire to learn, then email us your resumé. In lieu of a cover letter, let us learn about you by answering one of these questions (you choose):

  • What kind of environment do you thrive in?
  • What’s the biggest chance you’ve ever taken?
  • What is your favorite simple solution to a complicated problem?
  • What was the last thing you learned that is completely unrelated to your professional life?
  • How does the customer factor into your day to day role?
  • What is your definition of wow?

Open Positions

We are always willing to hear from you with expressions of interest for future employment opportunities.

If you wish to do so, please send details of your skills, work history and availability to careers@encore.dental
