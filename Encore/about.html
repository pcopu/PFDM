About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve access to dentistry by providing the highest quality non-clinical practice services to some of the nation’s leading dental practices.

We do this by affiliating with top dentists throughout the country, while providing excellent support services that help dentists better manage and grow practices. With our in-depth understanding of what it takes to run a successful dental practice – we strive to provide strategic support and top-notch services allowing dentists to do what they do best – practice dentistry.

Encore Dental’ comprehensive suite of dental practice support services are rated highly among dental support services organizations:

  • Discounted rates with leading labs & dental supply companies to help reduce costs and increase practice profitability
  • Hassle-free & easy payroll and benefits administration
  • Human resources consulting and support
  • Comprehensive review & recommendations of Payor agreements to improve negotiated rates & increase profitability
  • Streamlined and expert administration of practice finances
  • Expert management of facilities

Encore Dental also offers industry-leading compliance and auditing programs built around our proprietary Boomerang Practice Management (Electronic Health Records) systems to ensure quality care and transparency. Learn more about us!

Message From The Chairman and Founder

I am very enthusiastic about future in the dental space and what we are doing – for dentists, for patients, for dental staff – up and down the service chain.

I built a successful cardiology practice, one of the largest in the nation at the time, in Gulfport, Mississippi, and began investing in real estate through our company Encore Enterprises in the 1990's. Encore Enterprises is now a successful, fully diversified commercial real estate and investment firm with over $1 Billion in assets under management.

At Encore we are operators – hands on. We have built and infrastructure platform that runs efficiently and can pivot quickly as needed for the economy and for our affiliate companies. We succeed at what we do because we are disciplined, fair, completely transparent in our business dealings, and because we have built an overall culture that I am proud of and that is sustainable as we move forward.

As a physician first, I understand the priority of the oath for patient care. I have seen throughout my career that if you treat the patient right, the business will come. That is why we empower the dentist and dental staff so that patient care can be their choice.

These are truly exciting times. As a dentist, we hope you will visit with us about your opportunities to work with and partner with us. We look forward to seeing you!


Bharat H. Sangani MD

Why We Are Different

We are going to improve dentistry and access to dentistry by providing the highest value non-clinical dental services to market leading dental practices.

We do this by working with top dentists throughout the country, while providing excellent support services that help dentists better manage and grow practices. With our in-depth understanding of what it takes to run a successful dental practice – we strive to provide strategic support and top-notch services allowing dentists to do what they do best – practice dentistry.

By prioritizing and maximizing value for our partner dentists, Encore is building a premier brand in the dental industry. The value of patient care. The value of the dentist’s ownership and provider experience. And, the value of the practice. We are one of the only service organizations that not only allows our partner dentists to participate in the higher profits of a portfolio of practices, we ensure that our partner dentists are side-by-side with us and so we succeed together.

We focus on the business – You focus on being the very best Dentist

Meet The Leadership Team

Keith Miller serves as President and Chief Executive Officer for Encore Dental, leading the organization’s growth and all DSO operations. Mr. Miller has over 26 years of experience, a seasoned executive that has been successful leading high-growth platforms in both the retail and retail healthcare sectors. He fosters a servant leadership culture and has built high-performing teams wherever he has been.

Most recently, Mr. Miller led Legacy ER and Urgent Care as President of that DFW-based start-up healthcare business. He tripled the size of the organization in his first year through strategic acquisitions and implemented the platform and programs that allow the group to continue growth as a regional and national presence.

Mr. Miller spent 13 years as a principal leader in the growth of Dental One Partners (a top ten national Dental Services Organization). Starting with Dental One in 2002 when the DSO supported a small number of practices, Mr. Miller served as EVP and Chief Operating Officer as the company grew to north of 150 locations in 14 states during his tenure. He led many of the company’s key functional departments: operations, real-estate, construction, acquisitions, human resources, training, marketing and revenue recovery. Mr. Miller has a deep knowledge and understanding of the DSO space and is very excited to employ a revolutionary dentist friendly model with Encore.

Mr. Miller holds a Bachelors in Business Administration with a Marketing major from Ohio University.

Mr. Richardson leads financial and corporate development activities. He brings over 25 years of financial and operations experience, much of that helping small and early stage companies with growth, expansion, and change.

Expertise includes the following activities and industry sectors:

  • Private Equity
  • Real Estate
  • Management Consulting
  • Healthcare
  • Investment Banking
  • Technology
  • Operational Execution

Mr. Richardson’s healthcare projects include leading the expansion of an Ambulatory Surgery Center operation in Houston, Texas, adding successful new locations in the Southwest that doubled the ASC group’s net income from $11 million to approximately $25 million. That turnkey effort provided real estate services, licensing and credentialing, and equipment and operational financing. Other healthcare transactions include portfolio acquisitions of properties such as Baylor/USPI MOBs utilizing tax-advantaged TIC structures.

Through his activities in private equity and investment banking, Mr. Richardson has directed or materially participated in closing over $700 million in commercial real estate transactions, raising over $250 million in Reg D capital, and raising capital and managing over $60 million in private equity ventures.

As SVP of Acquisitions for Encore Dental, Mr. Davis is responsible for implementing the company’s growth strategy for acquisitions of practices and procurement of sites for de novo practices. He manages the transaction pipeline and oversees the due diligence and other processes up through closing.

Mr. Davis has been in the dental industry his entire professional career, beginning as a practice broker and going on to found and grow DSOs himself. Mr. Davis co-founded Family Dental, Inc. in 1995 and was a principal and executive within that organization for 20 years as it grew and merged into the group known today as Dental One Partners, a DFW-based DSO that is one of the top 10 nationally. Most recently he served as Vice President of Acquisitions for MB2 Dental Solutions, a regional DSO with over 70 practices.

Mr. Davis received his MBA from the University of Phoenix after obtaining his Bachelor of Business Administration from Northeast Louisiana University.

Ms. Garcia leads regional practice operations in Texas, managing practice P&Ls, business activities, and staff. Through years of hands-on experience in the dental industry, she has developed strong capabilities in financial analysis, budgeting, resource allocation, and financial performance, and has consistently improved the productivity of her practices.

Ms. Garcia’s experience includes senior operational positions with some of the larger Dental Service Organizations, including serving as Regional Operations Leader for Dental One and Deca Dental. With Deca Dental, she improved the performance of her practices by 275% year over year. Most recently, she was Director of Operations for Capstone Dental Services where she increased year over year performance by 20%.
