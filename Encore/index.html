The Evolution of Practice Ownership

Encore Dental Services owns and manages vibrant, thriving dental practices, partnering with community-based dentists so those providers can prioritize patient care. Great patient care drives everything, and our goal is to relieve our partner dentists of operations and practice management so that they can focus on a service environment built first and foremost for the patient experience. We go above and beyond a standard management company, providing multiple opportunities for our partner dentists to choose the path appropriate for them, whether based on stage of life or career. The dental industry is evolving and the landscape is changing. If you’re a solo practitioner, or even a small group practice, you will benefit from having strong partners on your side. Now is the time! Harvest the value you’ve created! Expand your opportunities. Reduce your risk.

Sell and Retain Material Ownership

Sell a portion of your practice to Encore and let us take care of the headaches of practice management. You can retain material ownership and participate in the benefits of added value and resources within a portfolio of practices.

Buy into a Practice Partnership

We believe in the partnership model, and so as we acquire practices where the selling dentist is retiring or otherwise transitioning out of the practice, there are attractive opportunities to be the new partner with us in that acquisition.

Sell Your Practice

Often, we purchase practices from dentists that are nearing retirement or for other reasons desire to sell the entire practice. We can assist doctors who want to walk away or sell and remain an employee.

Partner on Multiple Practices

As we stabilize a partnership practice, we can work together to add additional practices under the clinical direction and oversight of strong dentists that are entrepreneurial and have good management capabilities.

Become an Associate With Us

We provide multiple opportunities for our associates. Once you are part of the Encore family, you can choose the appropriate path for your stage of life or career, whether that is as an associate or growing into a partner with us.
