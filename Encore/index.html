The Evolution of Practice Ownership

Every dentist is faced with multiple opportunities throughout their career. A dentist may want to sell their practice, hand over business management, or find opportunities for ownership. These same professionals don’t always see a clear path to attaining their goals. Encore has developed different models that can serve a dentist at any place they may be in life.

Unlike most Dental Service Organizations (DSO’s) Encore has created several different options to help dentists maximize wealth creation. Encore surpasses simple partnership – we ensure a well-developed strategy to help each and every client achieve maximum value for their career opportunities.

Encore’s executive team has been very successful in the DSO space for over 15 years and has learned a lot about how to help our dentist clients achieve their goals. Please take an in-depth look into Encore. We know that the opportunities we can provide cannot be matched in the dental industry.

Sell and Retain Material Ownership

Partner with Encore and let us take care of the headaches of practice management. You can retain material ownership and participate in the benefits of added value and resources within a portfolio of practices.

Buy into a Practice Partnership

We believe in the partnership model, and so as we partner with selling dentists who may be retiring or otherwise transitioning out of the practice, there are attractive opportunities to be the new partner with us in that acquisition.

Sell Your Practice

Often, we partner with dentists that are nearing retirement or for other reasons desire to sell the entire practice. We can assist doctors who want to walk away or sell and remain an employee.

Partner on Multiple Practices

As we stabilize a partnership practice, we can work together to add additional practices under the clinical direction and oversight of strong dentists that are entrepreneurial and have good management capabilities.

Become an Associate With Us

We provide multiple opportunities for our associates. Once you are part of the Encore family, you can choose the appropriate path for your stage of life or career, whether that is as an associate or growing into a partner with us.
